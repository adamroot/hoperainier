Donate to Hope Rainier today!

Childhood cancer patients, along with their families, miss twice as many school days as other kids. Hope Rainier aims to solve this problem by providing a no-cost, high-impact, immune system-sensitive school for cancer patients and their families, and for those who suffer from other chronic medical conditions.

Hope Rainier will be working closely with Children’s Medical Center, Medical City Children’s Hospital, and Ronald McDonald House. However, we also need your help!

Help prevent children affected by cancer and blood disorders from getting behind in school by donating to Hope Rainier. Your generosity will go towards providing these children and their families with the education and support they need.
